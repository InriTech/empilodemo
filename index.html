    About Empilo Medical Centre

    Providing World Class Healthcare Services

    Your Trusted Healthcare Partner in Centurion

    No matter the stage or phase, Empilo takes great pride in providing families with healthcare evolving for life.

    Modern medicine has many triumphs, but its treatments for chronic and degenerative disease are not among them. Reliance on conventional drugs, surgery and radiation does little to address the causes of the condition, with the result that treatment too often only slows the progression of the disease – sometimes with very little benefit – and too often at a terrible cost in quality of life for the patient.

    At Empilo Medical Centre, we focus on comprehensive care that addresses the root causes of health issues while providing immediate relief and long-term wellness strategies.

    • Family-friendly medical environment
    • Treatment for both adults and children
    • Easy accessibility and convenient location
    • Emphasis on preventive healthcare
    • Shuttle service for Olievenhoutbosch area

    Our Specialized Services

    Comprehensive healthcare services for all your needs

    Women's Health

    Women, being the primary carers of their families in the majority of situations, both for children and elderly parents, need an extra dimension to their medical care. This requires recognition of their constant giving and caring.

    Men's Health

    We know how hard it is to make time to come to see the doctor. We are happy to talk about any aspect of Men's Health that may be of interest to you – not just men-only problems like prostates but also weight loss, smoking cessation, mood problems like depression or stress, heart health.

    Child Health

    At Empilo Medical Centre, we provide a high standard of care for your children, from the infant to the adolescent years. Our pediatric care focuses on growth, development, and preventive healthcare for young patients.

    Travel Health

    More and more people are travelling internationally than ever before, be it going on foreign holidays or travelling for business. Many destinations have health risks for travellers. Travellers will wish to remain well and illness free on their overseas trips.

    Preventive Health

    Looking at your level of health even when you are not ill is a valuable exercise. Perhaps you would like more get-up-and-go? Or try to turn the clock back a bit? Our preventive health services help you maintain optimal health.

    Prescriptions

    Prescriptions are handled with great care in this practice. We want to ensure you get the right medicines for you. Please submit requests for prescriptions in writing, to ensure errors are kept to a minimum.
